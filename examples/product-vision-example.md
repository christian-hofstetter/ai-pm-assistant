## Product Context (summary)

TaskFlow is a project management tool for marketing agencies with 10-50 employees. They juggle 20+ client projects simultaneously and struggle with visibility across campaigns, deadlines, and team workload.

## Vision Statement

**"Every marketing team delivers great work on time — without the chaos."**

## Vision Test (Roman Pichler's criteria)

| Criterion | Pass? | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-------|
| Inspiring | ✅ | Speaks to the emotional pain of chaos |
| Concise | ✅ | One sentence |
| Describes positive change | ✅ | "Great work on time" is the outcome |
| Ambitious but achievable | ✅ | Doesn't claim to solve all PM problems |
| Shared | ✅ | Relevant to POs, designers, account managers |
| Ethical | ✅ | No concerns |
| Enduring (5-10 years) | ✅ | The need for organized creative work won't disappear |

## Alternative Vision Statements

1. "Marketing teams spend their energy on creative work, not project admin."
2. "Every client project is delivered with confidence — on brief, on budget, on time."
3. "The operating system for agencies that refuse to drop balls."

## Recommendation

Option 1 (the main vision) is the strongest because it's emotional ("chaos") and outcome-focused ("great work on time"). Option 3 is punchy but might not resonate in formal stakeholder settings.
